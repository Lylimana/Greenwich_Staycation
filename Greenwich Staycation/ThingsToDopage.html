  • Things to do

You and your loved ones will never be bored with the wide range of activities on your doorstep.

Spend your day at Greenwich park and once you get a little peckish, head down to the market and choose from a plethora of cuisines provided by hawkers and vendors.

Take the kids to the variety of buffets and arcades at the O2 and round of the day with a spicy dinner date with your significant other at the many local restaurants.
